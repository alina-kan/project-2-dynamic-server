Exploration of Global Power Plants and Energy

By Lizzie Paterson, Andy Phan, and Alina Kanayinkal

 

The Global Power Plant Database is an open source database of many types of power plants around the world. It has lots of different information regarding the powerplants, such as the longitude and latitude of the plant, the country of origin, plant capacity, and more. It also covers around 35,000 power plants from 167 countries, focusing on thermal and renewable plants. We explored the locations of the power plants, the energy source used by the plant, and the plant capacity.
